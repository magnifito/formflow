Data Deletion Instructions

How to remove your data from Formflow.

User Data Deletion

Formflow respects your right to be forgotten. In accordance with GDPR and other data privacy regulations, you may request the deletion of your account and associated data.


Option 1: In-App Deletion

If you have access to your account:

  1. Log in to your Formflow Dashboard.
  2. Navigate to Settings > Account.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and click Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the action. This will permanently remove your account and all submission data.

Option 2: Deletion Request (Facebook/Meta)

If you used Facebook Login and wish to remove the connection and data:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account's "Settings & Privacy" > "Settings".
  2. Click "Apps and Websites".
  3. Find "Formflow" in the list.
  4. Click "Remove".
  5. This will trigger a data deletion request to our systems.

Option 3: Manual Request

You may also email us directly at privacy@formflow.fyi with the subject "Data Deletion Request". Please include your account email address. We will process your request within 30 days.
